The BYU women's cross country team clinched their sixth NCAA national championship on November 23, 2024, at the Zimmer Championship Course in Verona, Wisconsin. HERALD EXTRA This victory marked their first national title since 2020. WISCONSIN BADGERS The Cougars achieved a team score of 147 points, outperforming West Virginia, who secured second place with 164 points. WIKIPEDIA This triumph contributed to a historic sweep, as BYU became only the fifth program in NCAA history to win both men's and women's cross country team titles in the same year, a feat last accomplished by Colorado in 2004. BYU COUGARS The team's success was attributed to their unwavering dedication, strategic planning, and the leadership of head coach Diljeet Taylor. The Runner's Performance Dashboard is a comprehensive tool designed to track a runner's progress toward a marathon. It highlights key performance metrics such as Weekly Training Volume, which displays the total duration of all sessions, and Weekly Total Distance, showing miles covered over time. The Performance Model visually represents the session load, positive and negative responses, and overall performance trends as the runner approaches competition day. Additional insights include Weekly Time in Heart Rate Zones, helping to analyze time spent in various intensity levels, and Session Logs, which detail pace, training distance, and heart rate data. With clear visualizations like line charts and scatter plots, this dashboard provides a detailed yet user-friendly overview, empowering runners to monitor training efficiency, optimize performance, and prepare strategically for race day.
